Sources

This page reads the National Statistics Office data-availability workbook directly and recomputes every figure (status, coverage, freshness, per-goal and per-agency totals) each time it loads. UN custodian agencies, numeric targets and the improving/worsening direction are not in the NSO workbook; they come from a fixed reference table built into this page following the IAEG-SDGs custodian framework, and change only when a new indicator is added. The "national source" is the reporting body inside Mongolia (mostly the National Statistics Office). Progress interpretation is shown only where the desired direction is unambiguous.
